## v1.0.0

### Features

- `HtmxFormMixin` and `HtmxModelForm` for reactive Django forms driven by HTMX triggers
- Field-state helpers: enable/disable, required toggling, choice/queryset swapping, and resets
- `HtmxFormUpdateViewMixin` and `IsHtmxRequestMixin` for serving re-rendered form fragments
- Example Django application demonstrating usage
